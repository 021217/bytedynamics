Frequently Asked Questions

What’s Included with Unlimited Design?

With our unlimited design service, you don’t pay by the hour or per project. Submit as many design requests as you like, and we’ll work through them in your specified order. We produce at least 3 designs each day, not including revisions, with an average of 3 to 7 designs delivered daily.

What Does Unlimited Brand Access Mean?

Our unlimited plan allows you to request designs for multiple brands, not just one. Simply provide the briefing and materials for each brand, and our team will create designs that align with each brand’s unique style.

How Long Will It Take to Receive My Design?

After you submit your requests, our designer will start working on them based on your priorities. We promise to deliver the first draft within 12-24 working hours. Keep in mind that more complex designs (like company logos) may require additional time.

What Is Unlimited Revision?

You can request unlimited revisions on your designs until you’re completely happy. We’ll make any necessary changes based on your feedback until the design meets your expectations.

What Types of Designs Can I Request?

We offer a broad range of design services. Here are some of the most common requests:

  • Logos
  • Art & Illustrations
  • Social Media Graphics
  • Business Materials (Name Cards, Letterheads, Presentations, etc)
  • Print Media (Brochures, Banners, Packaging, etc)
  • Fashion Items (Clothing, Masking, Scarves, etc)
  • Web & Mobile Graphics (Digital Banners, E-commerce Banners, etc)
Are There Any Contracts or Hidden Costs?

There’s no long-term contract needed. That’s right—our plan is flexible and free from commitments. Example your monthly rate is RM2500, and you’ll have unlimited access to designs with no additional fees or hidden costs.
